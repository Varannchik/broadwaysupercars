RIDE ALONG EXPERIENCE

At Broadway SuperCars we realize that not everyone wants to drive, and sometimes it’s nice to just sit back and enjoy the scenery. Our Ride Along Experience is a luxury sports car rental with a twist, because we provide the driver so you can sit back and enjoy the ride. We created our Ride Along Experience to share our appreciation of exotic, luxury sports cars with drivers and non-drivers alike.

A Ride Along Experience makes a unique and thoughtful gift for that special someone that enjoys the thrill of a high-performance sports car, but may prefer to ride rather than drive. Delight that special person with the ride of a lifetime. Imagine the smile on their face when they climb into one of our Bentley, Lamborghini, Ferrari, Audi, Jaguar, or Aston Martin high-performance cars. Grandma’s 80th birthday, high school graduation, wedding anniversary, or just because - the gift of a Broadway SuperCar Ride Along Experience will create memories that last a lifetime! Or, of course you could just buy one for yourself…

We have a Ride Along Experience to fit most budgets, so please contact us to discuss the option that’s right for you.

WHAT TO EXPECT

Feel the wind in your hair, hear the rumble of a finely tuned engine, and smell the clean scent of soft leather as it wraps you in luxury. Words really can’t describe how it feels to cruise down the highway in one of our top luxury cars, but you get the idea. So sit back, relax, and enjoy the ride!

1 READY!

Your place or ours…On the day of your Ride Along Experience you can either stop by our luxury rental car New Jersey location, or we can pick you up.

2 SET!

Check out the ride. We go through an initiation on the car’s features and discuss what you’ll experience during the ride along.

3 GO!

Buckle your seatbelt, and we’re off for the ride along experience of a lifetime!

Choose from either our 30 or 45 minute Ride Along Experience. We have carefully selected our route to ensure you experience the full power and handling of these magnificent cars. Our professional, certified drivers take you on a cruise along highways and byways, hugging the curves of the road and opening her up along the straight-a-way. Feel how the car responds to acceleration, cornering, and braking and really appreciate the car at to the fullest. We’ll even play your favorite tunes.
