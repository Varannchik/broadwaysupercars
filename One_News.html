Hudson Mod Magazine Event / AUG’20


On June 26, 2012, HudsonMOD held its anticipated launch event at the Waterside Restaurant located along the picturesque Hudson River in North Bergen. More than 200 guests attended the soiree that was sponsored by Chanel, Bacardi, Grey Goose, Saks Fifth Avenue, Benzel Busch, Frank Gehry New York and Ecru New York. Guests enjoyed the HudsonMOD-jito and Papi Special for the night, while making memories at the event’s two photo booths, Ecru Braid Bar and Chanel touch-up station. One of the night’s highlights, however, was the jaw-dropping custom HudsonMOD cake made by Carlo’s Bakery that was almost too good looking to slice into.

​The always-stunning Daisy Fuentes turned heads as the magazine’s cover model made an epic appearance that stopped guests in their tracks. Joumana Kidd was on her A-game as the event’s hostess, welcoming V.I.P’s–including Derek Saathoff of A-List New York and fashion designer Randi Rahm–as they made their entrances. As the night came to an end, the sunset over the Hudson River was the perfect crescendo for an evening of luxury, and guests were able to enjoy time both indoors and outdoors, mingling and enjoying the cool breeze off of the water.
