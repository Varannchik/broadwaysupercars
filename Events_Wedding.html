Wedding Cars

If you’re looking for unique wedding ideas that will make a lasting impression on your guests, then let Broadway Supercars provide a dream car for your dream wedding. Limousines are so yesterday. Don’t show up in a run-of-the-mill limo when you can drive to your wedding in a modern day carriage. That Vera Wang wedding dress would look stunning paired with a red hot Audi Spyder, or Glacial White Bentley.

Why settle for just one Broadway SuperCar when you can rent the entire fleet? Talk about a photo op! Imagine a whole fleet of exotic cars waiting to whisk your wedding party to the rehearsal dinner, wedding venue or reception. You can arrive at the wedding in one car, then leave for the reception or honeymoon in a different vehicle. If you want a chauffeur driven Bentley to transport you to the wedding, and a Lamborghini to drive away in after the reception, we can do that! Get in good with the in-laws by sending a Broadway SuperCar to carry them to the wedding. So rent one, or all of our luxury, high performance cars for your special day. The options are only limited by your imagination.

A Broadway SuperCars rental is a distinctive gift for any occasion. Our gift cards make memorable bridal shower and wedding gifts.

We realize that the wedding day is all about the bride, but what about the groom? A Broadway SuperCars rental makes an exciting Grooms gift. So delight the groom by giving him a gift he’ll remember for a lifetime!

When you rent an exotic, high performance car from Broadway SuperCars you don’t just get a car, you get our super team of experts ready to assist in creating the wedding of your dreams. So please give us a call to discuss how we can help turn your fantasy wedding into a reality.

Broadway SuperCars specialize in modern Baraat wedding processions. Rather than riding in on one horse, picture the Groom arriving in a hand crafted car with 520 horses under the hood! Or imagine the delight on the faces of the barati when they see a fleet of supercars lined up for the procession.
