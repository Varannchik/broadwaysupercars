Broadway SuperClub

Whether you’re an individual that appreciates fine motor cars, or a corporation looking for an exceptional car service, a Broadway SuperClub membership is the right choice.

Imagine having a fleet of hand crafted motor cars at your disposal. Slide into the fine leather driver’s seat of an exquisite driving machine after a hard day’s work. See the pavement flying beneath your wheels, hear the engine purring in your ears, and feel the stress melting away.

Why not surprise and impress your clients by providing them with a superior, high-performance supercar to use during their visit? Or send one of our classy, sophisticated automobiles with a chauffeur to drive them around town.

Our attentive staff is at your service, so please contact us to discuss which SuperClub membership is right for you.

The Concept

Variety is the spice of life, and driving the same car day in and day out is borrr-ing! With a Broadway SuperClub membership you can choose any car you want to drive from our super fleet. Drive a Ferrari, a Lamborghini, an Aston Martin, a Jaguar, a Bentley, or one of the other cars in our collection whenever you like. Purchasing a Broadway SuperClub membership is like buying an entire fleet of luxury, exotic automobiles.

Why bother with the headaches that come with buying a high-end luxury car when you can have access to a whole fleet without the expense and nuisance of direct ownership? Belonging to the Broadway SuperClub is like owning an entire collection of exclusive automobiles, without the hassle of storing, insuring, and maintaining them!

IT’S BENEFITS

Broadway SuperClub members are part of an exclusive group of high performance car enthusiasts. Membership in the Broadway SuperClub has its privileges:

  • Anytime access – have access to the Super Fleet at your convenience.
  • Priority booking – SuperClub members receive priority when booking a SuperCar.
  • Free delivery – our expert team will deliver your choice of vehicle to your location.
  • White glove service – we treat our members like superstars, and our cars are maintained to the highest standards in the industry.

Please contact our super Staff to discuss how you can have a fleet of SuperCars at your disposal.

The Fine Print

  1. Membership expires 2056.

  2. A Corporate Member is an entity that receives a pool of sixty (60) driving passes per season that may be used by up to five (5) Corporate Member Designees (“Designee”), and each named Designee may drive as often as he/she likes (each day of use depletes the shared pool by one driving pass/day per person). Driving guests of the Designee(s) also deplete the shared pool by one driving pass/day/person, and guests (non‐members) are limited to driving twice/season. A Corporate Member may add Designees (above the initial 5) for an additional initiation fee and annual dues. Designees may be added, deleted, or changed each season by the Corporate Member.

    Six driver guest passes are included at no additional cost. Once all 6 driver guest passes are used, the Gold Member may purchase additional Qualified Driver Guest Passes at the then current rate for Gold Members and at the sole discretion of the Club.

  3. Silver Members may purchase up to five (5) Qualified Driver Guest Passes at the then current rate for Silver Members. The Silver Member may purchase additional Qualified Driver Guest Passes at the sole discretion of the Club, and the rate for additional passes, beyond the initial five, is at the then‐current Bronze rate (a/k/a, the retail rate.)

  4. Silver Members may purchase up to five (5) Qualified Driver Guest Passes at the then current rate for Silver Members. The Silver Member may purchase additional Qualified Driver Guest Passes at the sole discretion of the Club, and the rate for additional passes, beyond the initial five, is at the then‐current Bronze rate (a/k/a, the retail rate.)

  5. Bronze Members are not guaranteed the right to bring guests – driver(s) or non‐driver(s). The Bronze Member may purchase Qualified Driver Guest Pass(es) at the then current rate for Bronze Members (a/k/a, the retail rate) and at the sole discretion of the Club with prior approval. Non‐ Drivers, with preference for immediate family, may join the Bronze Member at the sole discretion of the Club with prior approval.

  6. Additional Qualified Driver Guest Pass(es) may be made available to the Corporate Member at the sole discretion of the Club and at the then current rate.

  7. The Club reserves the right to limit the maximum Non‐Driver Guests based on membership level, per day limits, and/or per guest limits.

  8. The Club may issue additional Non‐Driver Guest Passes at its sole discretion.

  9. Discounts apply to Corporate Events, Driving Schools, and Tastes of the Track.

  10. 3rd party event examples: Bentley Driving Event, Jaguar Experience, World Class Driving 200MPH program, Audi Experience, etc.

  11. VIP ticket examples: pit passes to professional races, such as Grand‐Am, American Le Mans, World Challenge, and IndyCar, suite access to future MMC‐hosted professional races, special concours events, etc.* Founding Members, who joined prior to the Club opening in July 2008, have the same privileges as Gold Members but may resell their memberships upon the Club reaching 350 members and also enjoy priority on services and/or reservations above all other membership classes.All references to “family” mean specifically: a spouse, sibling or step‐sibling, parent or grandparent, child or grandchild.
