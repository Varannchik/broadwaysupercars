FAQ RENTALS

What is the minimum age to rent a supercar?

All drivers must be at least 25 years of age.

How long can I keep the car?

Rental rates are based on a 24 hour period from the time of pick-up.

What does one day, week-end & week rental mean?

  • One day rental means 24 hours
  • Week-end is 3 days beginning Thursday, Friday or even Saturday
  • 1 week is 7 days

What documents shouldI bring with me?

  • You must present a valid driver’s license that includes a photograph at the time of rental.
  • A major credit card is required.
  • Proof of car insurance, and a copy of your Insurance declaration page.

What’s the best way to make a reservation?

The fastest way to make a reservation is through our online reservations form. We will contact you by phone to confirm your reservation and guarantee your vehicle of choice (barring unforeseen circumstances).

Alternately, you can give us a call and we will take all necessary information over the phone at 973-779-8405.

Can I cancel my reservation?

Yes, but you must notify us at least 72 hours prior to the scheduled pick-up or delivery time to avoid a charge of up to one day’s rental fee.

Do I need a credit card to rent a vehicle?

You must present avalid credit card that matches the name on the driver’s license. A credit card authorization is requested prior to rental and will be used as a deposit. This is an authorization, not a charge to your credit card. Authorizations vary from $ 250 to $ 500, depending on the car.

We accept Visa, MasterCard and American Express. Final payments will be charged to the authorized credit card. If a customer chooses; final payments can be paid in cash.

Will you accepta cash deposit?

Yes, cash depositsare accepted.

What fees does the State of New Jerseyimpose?

A 7% sales tax and a $2.05 per day road tax will be applied to all rentals.

What about the mileage charges?

You are allowed up to 100 miles per day. Additional mileage charges for most vehiclesare $6 per mile.

Can we have additional drivers?

One additional driver is permittedand all drivers must meet the same requirements (25 years of age, provide a valid driver’s license and insurance information).

What about delivery?

We deliver to airports, hotels, and most locations from New Jersey to New York City and surrounding areas. Please note there may be an additional delivery charge depending on the location.

Do you offer one way rentals?

We offer one way rentals that are based on the length of the rental.

Can we take the car out of state?

Yes. The following states are allowed: New Jersey, New York, Massachusetts, Vermont, New Hampshire, Maine, Rhode Island, Connecticut, Pennsylvania, Delaware, Maryland and Virginia.

Are the cars equipped with GPS or a Navigation System?

We provide Garmin GPS/Navigation System for cars that don’t already include an integrated system.

Can you providea child seat?

We can provide a child-seat if necessary.

Does the car come with an NJ EZPASS?

We provide NJ EZPASS on all cars in our super fleet.

Can we drive the car off-road?

SuperCars aren’t equipped to handle off-road driving, and in no circumstances should you take one of our cars on an unpaved road.

Can I smoke in the car?

NO! We don’t allow smoking in any of our supercars, and there will be a $250 cleaning fee deducted from your deposit if we find any evidence of smoking. You may also be liable for additional charges due to damage from burns or stains. Please review our Tour Policies for more details.

What should I do if there is an accident?

Call 911. Please ensure a police report is filed for insurance purposes and your protection. The Tour Manager will be with you to assist you through the process. We also include an accident kit with a disposable camera in each vehicle so you can take pictures of the damage and the scene.

What should I do if the car breaks down?

Our cars are perfectly maintained and in like new condition. However, even new cars can breakdown. In the unfortunate event of a breakdown, please contact your Tour Manager immediately. Absolutely do not attempt to make any repairs . Broadway Supercars reserve the right to replace the vehicle or increase the drive time of one or more of the other vehicles.

Do you offer short or long term leases?

We offer competitive rates for short term & long term rentals.

Do you allow pets in the car?

Pets are not allowed in the vehicle. If the vehicle is returned with pet dirt or odor, you will be charged an additional cleaning and deodorizing fee. The minimum charge is $50, but it could be higher based on the extent of the damage. Please be considerate. Thank You

Do I need to bring it back with a full gas tank?

We recommend you bring the car back with a full tank (93 Octane only). The fuel level is printed on the receipt at time of rental, and a surcharge will be applied if the vehicle is returned with less fuel than is printed on the receipt.

Can I rent a car by the hour?

Supercars can be rented by the hour, but there is a 4 hour minimum.

Can you provide a Chauffeur?

We offer professional chauffeurs at an additional cost for those that prefer to sit back and enjoy the ride.

What happens if I’m late returning the car?

What should I do if the car breaks down?

Our cars are perfectly maintained and in like new condition. However, even new cars can breakdown. In the unfortunate event of a breakdown, please contact your Tour Manager immediately. Absolutely do not attempt to make any repairs. Broadway Supercars reserve the right to replace the car or increase the drive time of one or more of the other vehicles.

FAQ for Gift Cards

Do Gift Cards expire?

Yes, Gift Cards expire 1 year from the date of purchase.

Is it possible to refund my Gift Card?

Gift Cards are non-refundable.

What do I do if my Gift Card is lost or stolen?

We will credit the balance remaining on the Gift Card with valid proof of purchase (the receipt that is sent with Gift Card). Please call us for assistance.

Do you charge any service fees for unused balancesleft ontheGift Card?

No, we don’t charge service fees for unusedGift Card balances.

Can I use my Gift Card if thecard is damaged?

Yes, as long as the serial number is legible. If the serial number isn’t legible, then we accept valid proof of purchase. Please contact us for assistance.

How long will the Gift pack take to be delivered?

If you order before 2pm and choose first class postage,we will usually dispatch the Gift Cards on the same day. Please allow a lead time of three to five business days. Orders placed after 2pm will usually be dispatched on the next business day. If you place your order prior to 2pm and chooseone-day delivery (for an extra charge), your Gift Card is guaranteed to arrive on the next business day before 1pm, anda signature will be required.

These delivery times and prices are only applicable to US delivery addresses; for delivery addresses outside the US, please contact us to discuss your requirements.Pleaseunderstand that we do our very best, but we can’t be held responsible for delays caused by the mail service.

Can I combine the values of two or more Gift Cards?

Yes, you can combine the value of two or more Gift Cards to purchase a SuperCar experience.

Can I extend my Gift Card?

A Gift Card is deemed to be invalid after the expiry date that is printed on the Gift Card. Under certain circumstances,a Gift Card can be extended, at our discretion, for a period of 3 months from the original expirationdate. There will be a $20 administration fee for extending the Gift Card. Please note that we can’t extend expiredGift Cards, and once a Gift Card has been extended it cannot be exchanged or refunded.

Can I have a refund?

We are happy to refund the cost of your Gift Card as long as the Gift Card is returned to us unused within 7 days of receipt. Please note that no refund will be given if you have used the Gift Card, or have already booked a SuperCar experience.

How long will my Gift Card take to arrive?

Our Standard Delivery service takes 3-5 business days to arrive, but it may take up to 15 working days. We recommend overnight delivery if you need the Gift Cardby a specific date. Please note that a signature is required for all overnight deliveries.

If you selected standard delivery and your Gift Card hasn’t arrived, please wait 15 business days before contacting us.

Can I amend my order, or send it to a different address?

If you contact us immediately,we’ll do our best to amend your order.Once your order has been shipped, we are unable to change any details of the order.

How do I find out about your latest experiences and special offers?

The fastest way to stay updated on our special offers is to sign up for our Newsletter. Our Newsletter contains updates about newest additions to our super fleet, and special offers. You can also check us out on Twitter and Facebook. Alternately, feel free to browse the ‘What’s New’ and ‘Special Offers’ sections on our website.

How can I give feedback following my experience?

We love feedback from our customers, so please take a moment to tell us about your experience. So please give us a call, send us an email, or complete our Contact Us form.

If you aren’t happy with any part of your experience,please bring it to our attention,so we can resolve it immediately. We’ve found that mostissues can be resolvedquickly when they are brought to our attention.

How do I become a Broadway SuperCar partner?

If you would like to become a partner/supplier / experience provider please contact our Sales Team– John Nouri at john@broadwaysupercars.com

How do paddle shift vehicles work?

Some of the cars used in the SuperCar Tour are equipped with hand controlled paddle shift. Paddle shift lets the driver change gears without removing their hands from the steering wheel. The Paddles are located right behind the steering wheel. The paddle to shift gears up is on the right, and the downshift paddle is on the left. Cars with paddle shift don’t have a clutch pedal. Alternately the driver may choose to use the“automatic” mode where the computer controls the gear changes.
