SuperCar Drive & NYC Helicopter Tour

Our Drive & Fly Tours are two thrill rides in one package! Enjoy the excitement of driving one of our luxury rental cars, and experience New York City as it’s meant to be seen; from the air! We combine driving a high-end exotic sports car with a private helicopter tour of New York City for the ultimate rush!

There is no better way to experience NYC than from the seat of a private helicopter! With our Drive & Fly Tour you’ll soar over the Statue of Liberty, Ellis Island, Yankee Stadium, and of course Broadway with your favorite person by your side.

Or take our Drive & Fly NYC Lights tour for a truly unforgettable view of New York after dark! See the lights of Broadway, the sparkle of neon in Time Square, and feel the somber beauty of the World Trade Center memorial. This is a truly unforgettable tour!

Our Drive & Fly tours include your choice of luxury rental car and a private helicopter tour for two. Broadway SuperCars is partnering with Pegasus Flight to create this exceptional adventure.

WHAT TO EXPECT

Drive Your adventure begins when you collect the SuperCar of your dreams from our location. Then you’re off to the heliport where your helicopter awaits.
Fly Board your private helicopter for your airborne site seeing tour of NYC.
Drive Relish the drive back to Broadway SuperCars in your fantasy car.
