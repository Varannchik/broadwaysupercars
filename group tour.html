SuperCar Tour

The Ultimate Driving Experience

So many cars, so little time! So what do you do when you can’t decide which car to drive? Well you drive them all of course! With the Broadway SuperCar Tour, you climb into the driver’s seat of 6 of the finest motor vehicles on the planet. Start your tour behind the wheel of our elegant Bentley Continental; next slide into the fine leather seat of a Nissan GT-R; then hug the road in our Lamborghini Gallardo Spyder, and you still have three more cars to go!

6 supercars in one tour

WHAT TO EXPECT

Feel the adrenaline rush of cruising in six different exotic motor vehicles in one day! You’ll follow a carefully selected route along some of the most scenic roads in the area. We’ve hand-picked these roads to provide you with just the right amount of curves and straight-a-ways so you can feel and appreciate what makes each car so special!

1 On your mark – We go over the safety and performance features of each vehicle. We show you how to shift gears, adjust mirrors, and buckle up for the ride of your life!
2 Get Set – We collect your first dream car. Feel your pulse pounding when you start the engine!
3 Drive – Hear the engine roar and feel the wind in your face. You’re off on a trip that will take your breath away!
4 And Switch – Are you having fun yet? Well the excitement has just begun! We take a short break for a photo op so you can capture the moment for posterity. Then you climb into your next car to begin another thrilling driving adventure!

Choose from either our 30 or 45 minute Ride Along Experience. We have carefully selected our route to ensure you experience the full power and handling of these magnificent cars. Our professional, certified drivers take you on a cruise along highways and byways, hugging the curves of the road and opening her up along the straight-a-way. Feel how the car responds to acceleration, cornering, and braking and really appreciate the car at to the fullest. We’ll even play your favorite tunes.
